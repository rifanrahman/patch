  • What is Rent Tracking

    Rent Tracking is a new way of boosting your credit history. We track your rent payments to your landlord or agent. If you pay on time, we report this to the credit reference agency. Experian and the add this information to your credit report...

  • How does Rent Tracking work?

    It's very easy. Sign up to patch. Connect to your bank account. Pay your rent. Patch reports your rent payments to Experian. Your credit history improves.

  • How do I see what you've tracked?

    You'll start recieving the benefit of Rent Tracking as soon as you sign up. You won't actually be able to view the information in your Experian credit report until around three months after signing up...

  • How much does Rent Tracking cost?

    Our Rent Tracking service is free to use

  • How does Rent Tracking improve my credit history

    Your credit history improves each time a new, positive record is added to your credit report such as making a payment on time. Your rent is a now qualifying payment.

  • What if I change property

    Not a problem. It's easy to continue Rent Tracking. Log into your Patch account after you make your last rent to your old property and let us know about your new place...
